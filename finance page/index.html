Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum down payment required?

The minimum down payment varies depending on the financing plan. Typically, it's around 10-20% of the vehicle's price.

Can I refinance my loan later?

Yes, refinancing is an option that may help lower your interest rate or change your loan terms.

What happens if I miss a payment?

Missing a payment may result in late fees and could affect your credit score. It's essential to communicate with your lender if you're facing difficulties.

Financing Glossary

APR: Annual Percentage Rate, the annual rate charged for borrowing.
Principal: The amount of money borrowed or the amount still owed on a loan.
Loan Term: The length of time you have to repay the loan.
